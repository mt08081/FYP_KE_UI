How This System Works
Data Source

The system uses 997 synthetic fault records based on K-Electric's Korangi, Surjani, Nazimabad, and Clifton areas with weather and kunda (illegal connections) risk data.

Fault Type Prediction

The ML model predicts what type of fault is likely (Motor Failure, Short Circuit, Leak, or Sensor Fault) based on plant location, weather, and kunda risk levels.

Restoration Time

A regression model estimates repair duration considering fault type, hour of day, weather conditions, and the wind×kunda risk interaction.
